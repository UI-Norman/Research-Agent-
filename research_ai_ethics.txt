AI Ethics and Responsible AI Development

Summary:
This research explores the ethical challenges in artificial intelligence, including bias, accountability, transparency, and human oversight.

Key Points:
- AI systems can inherit human biases from training data.
- Explainable AI (XAI) helps in improving transparency.
- Ethical frameworks are essential for AI governance.
- Human-in-the-loop systems ensure responsible decision-making.

Sources:
- Stanford Human-Centered AI Report 2023
- OECD Principles on AI
- EU AI Act Proposal

Conclusion:
Responsible AI design requires collaboration among technologists, policymakers, and ethicists.
